Surmusings

Rhymes, Reveries and Reminiscence

From Surbhi to her beloved grandmother Laxmi Devi Mehrotra, who introduced her to poetry


I will see you there
In the first winter dew
In the spring full of dreams of you
In the summer that brings me closer to you
In the autumn fire that burns between me
and you ...

- Surbhi Mehrotra
